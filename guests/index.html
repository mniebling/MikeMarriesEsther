Beacon Castle Visitors Guide

Nespresso machine

  1. Make sure the water reservoir on the back is filled. If not, fill it with fridge water (makes better coffee than tap water).
  2. Choose your flavor from the rack next to the machine. Red is the decaf option.
  3. Pop the capsule in the top of the machine — it only fits one way — and close the lid.
  4. Tap one of the two buttons on the top of the machine. The lights will blink; this means the machine is warming up.
  5. When the lights go solid, press the left button for a short coffee or the right button for a long.
  6. There's also a hot water kettle on the counter for tea or hot chocolate.
